02 — The Neural Brain

Claude thinks about your home every 5 minutes

A neural brain powered by Claude Sonnet and local Ollama models uses machine learning to control devices based on its training. It analyzes sensor patterns, camera feeds, and 30-day behavioral baselines to detect anomalies, predict what should happen next, and act on it.

📊

Anomaly Detection

Every 15 minutes, compares current sensor readings against 30-day rolling baselines per camera, per hour, per day-of-week. Flags statistical outliers.

0
💡

Room-Aware Hunches

Every 5 minutes, correlates motion sensors across 17 rooms with time-of-day patterns. "Lights left on in kitchen with no motion for 30 min → suggest turn off."

0
🎯

Learns & Adapts

The brain trains on your family's patterns — when you wake, when lights go on, when doors lock. It builds per-device, per-hour, per-day-of-week models and uses them to control your home automatically.

0
👤

Person Recognition

Face encodings + Claude vision identify family members at cameras. Triggers person-specific automations — arrival lighting, notifications, visit tracking.

05 — Voice & Chat Control

Talk to your house

Natural language commands through Alexa, Jarvis chat, or voice input. Entertainment, security, lighting, blinds, and scenes — all from a single sentence. The AI understands context and maps commands to the right devices.

"Goodnight"
Locks the front door, turns off all lights, shuts every blind, arms the system — one word puts the whole house to sleep
🌙
"Lock the front door"
Jarvis confirms and engages the smart lock. High-risk action — always verified before execution
🔒
"Turn off all the lights"
Every Hue bulb and Lutron switch across all 17 rooms goes dark in one command
💡
"Close the blinds"
All 6 SwitchBot motorized blinds in the Den, Dining Room, and Study close simultaneously
🪟
"Evening dim"
Turns on only lamps and dims them — overhead lights off, ambient mood lighting throughout the house
🕯️
"Play Disney"
Wakes Apple TV → CEC powers Samsung → Disney+ launches automatically — voice to streaming in 8 seconds
🏰
"Play Netflix"
Same seamless chain — full entertainment stack from any Echo in the house to the den TV, zero remotes
🎬
"Play YouTube"
Voice to YouTube on the big screen — Apple TV CEC handles power, input, and app launch
▶️
Automatic: Door opens after dark
Front porch lights and string lights turn on automatically when any exterior door opens and it's dark outside
